Welcome to Hyderabad Live

We are indeed privileged to extend you invitation to the 5th conference of Hyderabad Live (2017) at the Historical city of Hyderabad. The theme this year is "Left Main Interventions". We urge you to block your dates, take time off your busy schedules and join us to interact and update

The two day academic bonanza is not to be missed.

We look forward to welcoming you to the "Pearl City"
